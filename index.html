About Me


Hi, I'm Alyson. Nice to meet you.

So far, I am very much enjoying learning a whole new career, with new languages and systems. I am hoping to find a path for my future self that shares a passion for my work as well as the autonomy to pursue hobbies and spend time with my friends and family outside of my career.

My past experience.

For almost a decade, I have been an Event Planner in a variety of different industries. It has always been my goal to create the most memorable experience for my clients and everyone in attendance. There's nothing better than a sea of smiling faces when an event goes as planned.

For the last 2 years, I have been an event planner for Posh Petals Floral. This means I get to work with couples on one of their most important days: their wedding day! It's an honor to orchestrate the perfect day for them, so they are able to relax and enjoy the moments, their families, and their friends.

Prior to that, I worked for iHeartMedia as an Events and Promotions Director for 5 years. This is where I really got my bearings as an event planner. I had the opportunity to plan everything from large scale outdoor festivals, to smaller and more intimate events. Not only did I learn a lot in those 5 years, but I also created many life-long friendships that I cherish to this day.

For a full list of my professional adventures, check out my LinkedIn.
